Help

This is an application for the practice of English speech via Google Speech Recognition API:

  • Push the "Record" button and hold it while you read aloud a phrase on the screen.
  • The voice will be recognized via Web Speech API and you will see differences between required and recognized phrases.
  • Listen to your voice by clicking on the green "Listen" button after the app has detected the defects in your pronunciation.

Keep in mind
Recognition does not always work well.
Some phrases can be recognized incorrectly even with perfect pronunciation. If you have attempted 3 times without success, use the arrow button to go to the next phrase.

Recommendation
Use a high-quality headset with microphone to improve recognition.
